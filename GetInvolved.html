Memberships

Membership at any level provides access to a real Amuay wildlife experience all year long! See Amuay Wildlife Park like never before with exceptional membership benefits and privileges.

By becoming an annual member at Amuay Wildlife Park, you not only take advantage of a great value that pays for itself in as few as two visits you help us sustain the wildlife sanctuary.

Passholder benefits:

  • Unlimited FREE daytime admission to Amuay Wildlife Park for one full year
  • One free ‘Feed-a-Tiger’ experience with each visit
  • 10 % off all gift shop purchases
  • 10 % off off food purchases at the Jungle Hut Cafe
  • 10 % off off annual summer ‘Campout at the Park’
  • Discounted admission at participating zoos and parks
  • Special invitations to exhibit previews and members-only appreciation events
  • Free access to all daily shows
