Welcome to our Park

Want to see the best Venezuela has to offer in natural beauty?

You’ve come to right place, here at Amuay Wildlife Park we offer you the chance to see some of the most beautiful plants and animals in existence all in one place.

In 1970, the park was first opened by then president Rafael Caldera, since then it has grown to be one of Venezuela’s top tourist attractions attracting record numbers of visitors in the years after its redevelopment in 2008.

It’s clear why the park has become the Paraguana Peninsula’s most popular tourist attraction, with the park stretching over 72 km2 including varying terrains of grasslands, marshes and hills each with their own unique scenic beauty.

Not only are the views breath-taking, but the park is home to some of the most interesting Lions, Monkeys, Crocodiles and Birds on the planet all in the confines of their natural habitat.

With our scheduled tour we’ve carefully designed a bus route through the park which covers all the animals and plants providing picturesque photo opportunities with every turn.
