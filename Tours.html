Private Tours and VIP experiences
Looking for a way to impress your date or for a special gift for a special friend? We got you covered!
In those tours, guests will be escorted through the drive-through preserve with one of our knowledgeable guides who will tell you about the animals and take you to two behind-the-scenes areas (subject to availability), which could include: touching a tortoise, getting a closer view of the lions, meeting a rhino, or feeding a giraffe. The tour lasts 1-1/2 hours; after your tour, you can explore Safari World on your own or with your company.

Camping Trips

Amuay Wildlife Park is now offering Camping.

After a day at the park, return to an exclusive enclosed camp, surrounded by African wildlife. Under the cool canvas of your own private tent, you’ll get the full experience of camping in the wild, listening to the sounds of the different animals this is an immersive experience to say the least!
You have the option to either bring your own tent and pitch it or hire one of ours. Either way staff will be on hand to assist you in case you face any difficulty installing it. You can book one tent for a getaway (or speak to us about reserving a section of our campsite for a secluded private event). You will have access may staff members who are available to help you with anything you may need during your stay.
This exclusive enclosed campground is surrounded by the park’s wildlife animals (non-predatory only), providing the opportunity for up close encounters, viewings, and a night-time soundtrack of the Venezuelan Savannah come to life.
